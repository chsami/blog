About Me

Value First

I'm a software developer who thrives in the details. My world revolves around .NET, Java, databases, and increasingly AI, though I'm just as likely to be found reverse-engineering something to understand how it really works under the hood.

I believe good software comes from asking the right questions: How does this actually work? What happens at the edges? Will this scale? I'm drawn to clean architecture, well-considered patterns, and pragmatic solutions over clever ones.

When I'm not building, I'm contributing to open-source projects and thinking about how to make tooling and workflows better for everyone. There's something satisfying about solving a problem once in a way that helps many.

If you're looking for someone who prefers direct answers, structured thinking, and getting things do, let's connect.
